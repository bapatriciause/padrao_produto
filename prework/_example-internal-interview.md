# Internal Interview

If possible, interview front-line team members who work with and understand customer pain points: **sales reps, customer support, marketing, professional services, implementation teams**, etc. These people hear customer frustrations every day and often have insights that don't make it into formal feedback channels.

For guidance on asking the right questions and avoiding bias, see: [https://www.designkit.org/methods/interview.html](https://www.designkit.org/methods/interview.html)

> Ahead of the accelerator, capture the interview content and evidence only.
> Don't worry about perfect formatting, we can tidy and analyse it later.
