# Product Ideas

Capture the product problems and idea seeds you already have. Keep this grounded
in known customer pain, existing evidence, or current team hypotheses.

> Ahead of the accelerator, do not use this file to run an ideation exercise.
> Record existing ideas only and keep open questions explicit.

## Idea #1: [Short Idea Name]

### The Problem

- **Who has this problem?** [Specific user, buyer, operator, or team]
- **What is the problem?** [Describe the friction, delay, risk, or cost]
- **How big is the problem?** [Frequency, severity, revenue impact, support cost, compliance exposure, or churn risk]
- **How do they work around it today?** [Manual process, spreadsheet, extra headcount, exporting data, etc.]

### The Idea

- **What would the AI-powered solution do?** [Describe the product behavior in plain language]
- **What type of AI is involved?** [Classification, search, summarization, extraction, forecasting, generation, agent workflow, etc.]
- **What would the user experience look like?** [Step-by-step flow]

### Data Requirements

- **What data would the AI need?** [List systems, content, events, or records]
- **Do you have this data today?** [Yes / Partially / No]
- **Any data quality or access concerns?** [Missing fields, inconsistent schemas, permissions, history gaps, etc.]

### Expected Impact

- **For the customer:** [Time saved, better decisions, lower risk, higher conversion, reduced frustration]
